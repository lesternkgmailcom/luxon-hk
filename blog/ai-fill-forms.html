I forwarded two blank forms. My AI filled, signed, and stamped them.

Every year, shipping line partners send blank POA and Affiliate forms for renewal. This time, I forwarded the email to my AI. Minutes later, both forms were filled, signed, stamped, and ready to send.

April 2026 · 2 min read

It's that time of year again. Our shipping line partner sent an email with two blank forms: a Signing Agent Power of Attorney (POA) and an Affiliate Confirmation Letter — both for the 2026-2027 season. Last year's completed version was attached as reference.

What I used to do

Open last year's PDF. Open this year's Word doc. Go through each field one by one — company name, address, authorized signatory, S/C number, contact details. Copy carefully. Miss one field, and the form gets bounced back. Then print, sign, stamp, scan, email. About 30 minutes per form.

What I did this time

I forwarded the email to my AI with one line:

"Fill in these 2 forms with last year's as reference. Apply signatures. Send me the output in PDF."

That's it. My AI:

  1. Read both documents — identified the blank Word templates and last year's filled PDF
  2. Cross-referenced every field — copied company details, signatory info, and all mandatory fields from last year
  3. Applied my signature and company chop — pulled from its stored memory
  4. Verified all fields — double-checked nothing was missed
  5. Exported as PDF — ready to send, professional quality

From forward to finished: under 5 minutes. I reviewed the output — every field correct, signature placed properly, company chop aligned. Ready to send.

Time saved: ~55 minutes

Fields filled: 30+ across both forms

Errors: 0

Why it matters

This isn't a one-off trick. In international logistics, form-filling is constant — POAs, affiliate letters, customs declarations, shipping instructions, Bill of Lading reviews. Every partner has their own format. Every year needs updating.

The AI doesn't just copy-paste. It understands context: which fields carry over from last year, which ones need updating (dates, reference numbers), and which ones should stay blank per instructions (like S/C numbers).

Who is this for?

  • Logistics & procurement — annual POA renewals, affiliate confirmations, shipping instructions
  • Trading companies — customs forms, certificate of origin, commercial invoices
  • Compliance teams — regulatory filings, KYC documents, license renewals
  • Anyone who fills forms — if you've ever spent 30 minutes copying data from one PDF to another, this is for you

Not just filling

The same AI also:

  • Extracts key data from incoming forms and updates our database
  • Flags when forms are due for renewal
  • Archives completed forms in organized folders
  • Drafts cover emails when sending forms back to partners

It's not just faster — it's more reliable. No missed fields. No typos. No "please re-submit, section 4 is incomplete."
