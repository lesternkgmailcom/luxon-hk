I send a PDF to my AI. It signs and returns it.

During Asia/US contract season, I need to sign multiple contracts every day. My AI handles it — using my saved signature, it signs PDFs and sends them back in seconds.

April 2026 · 2 min read

It's contract season. Between Asia and US time zones, contracts come in at all hours — supplier agreements, NDAs, service contracts. On a busy day, I might need to sign 10-15 documents.

Here's what I used to do: download the PDF, open Preview, add my signature, save, rename, attach to reply email, send. Maybe 3-4 minutes per contract. On a heavy day, that's close to an hour just signing things.

What I do now

I forward the contract PDF to my AI assistant with a simple message:

"Sign this contract and send it back."

That's it. My AI:

  1. Receives the PDF — I forward it via email or WhatsApp
  2. Opens and reads it — confirms it's a contract, not spam
  3. Applies my saved signature — stored securely, placed in the right position
  4. Returns the signed PDF — ready to send, often with the reply already drafted

From start to finish: about 15 seconds. No downloading, no printing, no scanning.

The setup

This isn't magic — it's a configured workflow. During setup, I:

  • Saved my digital signature once (PNG with transparent background)
  • Told my AI where signatures typically go on contracts I receive
  • Set it to always flag contracts with unusual terms before signing

After that, it just works. Every time.

Time saved per month: ~5 hours

Contracts signed this season: 40+

Mistakes: 0

Who is this for?

Anyone who signs documents regularly:

  • Procurement managers — supplier contracts, purchase orders
  • Business owners — NDAs, service agreements, lease renewals
  • Real estate agents — tenancy agreements, sale & purchase forms
  • Consultants — engagement letters, scope of work documents

If you're signing more than 5 contracts a week, this workflow alone pays for itself.

Not just signing

The same AI also:

  • Summarizes contract terms before I sign
  • Flags unusual clauses or expiry dates
  • Organizes signed copies into folders automatically
  • Sends reminders before contracts expire

It's not just faster — it's smarter than doing it manually.
