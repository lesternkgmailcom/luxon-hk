Let AI Write Its Own Instructions

Don't know what to tell your AI? Ask it to figure it out for you.

The AI knows what it needs better than you do. Let it write its own instructions — then refine them together.

1

The meta-prompt

Instead of struggling to write perfect instructions, tell the AI what you're trying to accomplish and ask it to create its own guidelines. Example: "I write weekly sales reports. Help me write the best custom instructions so you can help me faster."

2

Refine together

The AI will generate a set of instructions. Read them — they'll be surprisingly good. Then tweak: remove what doesn't fit, add details it missed, and adjust the tone. Two heads are better than one.

3

Save and reuse

Once you're happy with the instructions, save them to your project (Lesson 2) or custom instructions. Every future conversation starts with this context — no more repeating yourself.

Before

"Make a report. Make it good. I don't know, just make it look professional."

After

"Help me write instructions so you can generate my weekly sales report perfectly every time."

💡
Pro tip: This works for any recurring task — emails, social media posts, meeting notes. The AI has processed millions of examples. Let it tell you what it needs.
