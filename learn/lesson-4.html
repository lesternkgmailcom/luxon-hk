The Copy-Paste Trick

Upload an example. Say 'do it like this.' That's it.

Paste one good example. The AI clones the format perfectly — every time.

1

Find a good example

Grab a past piece of work that turned out great — an email, report, invoice, social post. It doesn't need to be perfect. It just needs to show the style and structure you want.

2

Upload it to the chat

Drop the file directly into the chat, or paste the text. Claude and ChatGPT both handle PDFs, docs, and pasted text well.

3

Say "do it like this"

Three words is all you need: "Fill out this form using this as a template." The AI will match the tone, layout, and structure automatically.

Before

"Write me a report with a title at the top, then an executive summary in bold, then 3 sections with bullet points, using formal language and keeping it under 2 pages..."

After

"Do it like this." (3 words + your example)

💡
Works with anything: Invoices, emails, reports, social posts, meeting notes, contracts. If you have a good example, the AI can replicate the format.
