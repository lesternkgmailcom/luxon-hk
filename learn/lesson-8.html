Search Your Past Chats

Everything you've ever asked is still there. Here's how to find it.

Your AI remembers everything. Don't re-ask — just search. It's like having a second brain that never forgets.

1

Nothing is lost

Every conversation you've had with your AI is saved. Every question, every answer, every document you uploaded. It's all still there — you just need to know how to look.

2

How to search

ChatGPT: Use the search bar at the top of the sidebar. Claude: Projects keep all related chats together — search within a project. Tip: Search for keywords from your original question, not the topic.

3

Ask the AI directly

In the same project or conversation thread, just ask: "What do you remember about [topic]?" The AI can reference its own history and give you a summary. No manual searching needed.

💡
Pro tip: Projects (Lesson 2) make search 10× easier. Instead of searching all your chats, search within one project — all related conversations are already grouped together.
